## üé® Design System

### Color Scheme
**Light Mode:**
- Primary: `#007bff` (Blue)
- Success: `#28a745` (Green)  
- Warning: `#ffc107` (Yellow)
- Danger: `#dc3545` (Red)
- Background: `#f8f9fa` (Light Gray)
- Cards: `#ffffff` (White)

**Dark Mode:**
- Background: `#121212` (Dark)
- Cards: `#1e1e1e` (Dark Gray)
- Secondary: `#2a2a2a` (Medium Gray)
- Text: `#ffffff` (White)
- Muted: `#adb5bd` (Light Gray)

### Typography
- **Headers**: Bold, 18-24px
- **Body**: Regular, 14-16px
- **Captions**: 12px
- **Font Weight**: 400-700

### Spacing
- **Margins**: 15-20px
- **Padding**: 10-20px
- **Border Radius**: 8-25px
- **Card Spacing**: 15px between cards

### Shadows & Elevation
- **Light Mode**: Subtle shadows with 0.1 opacity
- **Dark Mode**: Stronger shadows with 0.3 opacity
- **Elevation**: 2-8 levels
